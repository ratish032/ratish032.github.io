Interview Agent

Zoom & Google Calendar Sync - Seamless Meeting Management

About

Interview Agent is a productivity application that integrates Zoom with Google Calendar to provide seamless meeting synchronization. The app uses OAuth 2.0 to securely authenticate users and access only their authorized meeting data.

Key Features

  • Automatically sync Zoom meetings with your Google Calendar in real time
  • Create new Zoom meetings directly through the integration
  • Reflect meeting changes instantly across connected calendars
  • Remove cancelled meetings from your calendar automatically
